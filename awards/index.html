DADABHAI NAOROJI INTERNATIONAL SOCIETY

It May be mentioned that Dadabhai Naoroji International society has also established “Dadabhai naoroji millennium award” and “Dadabhai naoroji international award”. It is given to those distinguished persons who have made significant achievements and contribution in their respective field of work, Namely Art,Culture,Education,Politics,Social work,Business and industry,Philanthropist,Scientist and any other field related to mankind. A 18 member jury comprising of distinguished persons from England and India select the deserving candidates after thoroughly scrutinizing the resumes and nominations received, To be ratified by the board of governors of Dadabhai Naoroji international society. It takes three months time to declare the awardees from the date of receiving the nominations. Distinguished and eminent persons like Governors, Academicians,Member of Parliament, Member of Legislative Assemblies, Eminent Lawyers, Philanthropist, Professors and Lecturers, Eminent social workers, Eminent business persons and so on can nominate the awardees. It may be mentioned that the candidate can also apply on their own by sending their resumes at [email protected]
